Built for the long sit.

The Aria is our own-range task chair, designed around one idea: a chair you configure once and forget about for a decade. A breathable recycled-polyester mesh back keeps air moving through long afternoons, while the moulded foam seat holds its shape past 60,000 sitting cycles in our supplier's fatigue testing.

Every mechanism is specified on this page, every fabric is priced in the open, and the same number applies whether you are buying one chair or fitting out a floor. That is how the whole site works.

Why the Aria over a lookalike

Most chairs at this price hide a 2 year mechanism warranty behind a 5 year headline. Ours is 5 years on everything mechanical, in writing, on the warranty sheet in the downloads tab. The gas lift is a Class 4 unit, the castors are 65mm twin-wheel, and the tilt is a genuine synchro mechanism rather than a basic rocker.
